White Water Rafting

Welcome to Splash — White Water Rafting! We are a team of passionate adventurers dedicated to providing thrilling and safe rafting experiences. Our mission is to connect people with nature while promoting environmental stewardship and outdoor recreation. From first-timers to seasoned paddlers, we have a trip tailored just for you.

History

Founded in 2005, Splash White Water Rafting has been leading the way in adventure tourism. Our founders, lifelong river enthusiasts, set out to share the thrill of the rapids with the world. From humble beginnings with a single raft and a dream, we have grown into a full-service outfitter serving hundreds of guests each season.

Over the years, we have expanded our services and now offer a variety of rafting trips suitable for all skill levels. We have earned a reputation for safety, professionalism, and unforgettable adventure. Our certified guides bring decades of combined river experience to every trip, ensuring every guest returns home with stories worth telling.
